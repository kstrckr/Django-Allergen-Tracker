Welcome to Allergen Tracker!

This site is intended to be used as a tool for new parents who have begun introducing solid foods to their baby's diet.

The top 8 allergens are recommended to be introduced at least 3 separate time and it's important to space them out so any reactions can clearly be tied to a single allergen.

Note-taking after each meal to log that an allergen has been introduced and for documenting any redness or suspected reactions can prove helpful as a family works through the list of all eight! The log can also be helpful at the baby's next doctors appointment so the pediatrician can stay up to date on what has been introduced and make not of any reactions.
